First Week Takeaways

Choosing code framework is like choosing what material to build your house in. Should you choose brick, plaster, concrete, etc? Each has its tradeoffs and it depends how long you are living in the house and where you are living.

Most importantly, if you have a builder you trust and respect, use what s/he knows how to build... Ruby it is!
